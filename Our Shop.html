The renovation happened around 1984, when the local home was turned into a flower shop. We welcome you to stop in and see the change for yourself!



The Flower Junction was established in 1986 by renovating a historic house on the corner of Nassau Road and Main Street in New Delhi,India.
The charm of this old house is an ideal setting for our family owned and operated full service florist.
